Chips Recipe

Description

Chips is the most favorite fast foods in Kenya and is available at a cheap price. It is can be taken at any other time of the day!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • Bunch dhania
  • 1 tsp garam marsala
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Chili tomato sauce

Steps/Procedure

  1. Peel and chop potatoes the french fry way
  2. Boil the potatoes on low heat till halfway done. Drain hot water and run cold water to stop cooking process. Place in fridge ready to be deep fried.
  3. Meanwhile chop your vegetables ready for making masala.
  4. Deep fry chips till crispy and a bit golden.
